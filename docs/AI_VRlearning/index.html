VR AI Learning Tool - Unity Venice Demo

Our VR AI Learning Tool showcases a fully interactive Venice environment built in Unity, highlighting our capabilities in 3D modeling, physics simulations, realistic lighting, and immersive animation. This detailed virtual world demonstrates how we craft high-fidelity VR experiences that let learners explore iconic environments while practicing language skills.

Through this Unity model of Venice, we demonstrate not just technology proficiency, but also our commitment to creating meaningful, user-centered learning experiences. By merging VR, natural voice interaction, and gamified design, we aim to enhance motivation, immersion, and practical skill-building for language learners worldwide.

Additionally, we have explored the deployment of IBM Watson Assistant. You can try it in the bottom right corner, but this is not yet a fully completed version. We hope that once selected, we can continue development and bring it to full implementation.

You can Find the IBM Assistant Chatbot at the right bottom of the page to ask any questions.
