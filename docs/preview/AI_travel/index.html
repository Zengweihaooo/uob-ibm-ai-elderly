AI Travel Project

Our AI Travel project explores an AI-enhanced platform where users can share travel experiences, connect over common interests, and receive personalized trip recommendations. Leveraging IBM Watson Assistant, we aim to create an interactive travel chatbot that enriches each user's journey with relevant suggestions and community insights.

This project highlights our enthusiasm for data-driven personalization and user-centered design, ensuring a seamless experience for travelers worldwide. We look forward to expanding its functionality through collaborative research and real-world deployment.
