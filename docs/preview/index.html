University of Bristol & IBM Summer Projects

We are MSc Computer Science students at the University of Bristol, excited to collaborate with IBM on innovative summer project opportunities. Below are three project briefs focusing on VR, AI applications, and supporting diverse user groups.

AI for the Elderly

Design a mobile-based “Social Pet” that combats isolation among the elderly, encourages daily routines, and offers companionship. Harness IBM Watson Assistant, Speech to Text, and Text to Speech to foster an emotionally rich interactive experience while providing practical support.

Learn More

VR AI Learning Tool

Create an immersive VR adventure game set in a famous tourist destination like Venice, helping players learn a new language through real-world scenarios. Integrate IBM Watson for speech recognition and AI-driven conversations with in-game characters.

Learn More

AI Travel

Develop an AI-enhanced platform where users can share travel experiences, connect over common interests, and get personalized trip recommendations based on their profiles. Embed IBM Watson Assistant to power an interactive travel chatbot.
